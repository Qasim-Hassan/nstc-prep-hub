General NSTC Pipeline Information

  • Syllabus: F.Sc Part 1 books are highly recommended as the baseline for every subject test.
  • Stage 1 (Screening): A nationwide screening test is held first.
  • Stage 2 (Camp 1): The top 50 to 80 participants are selected for the first training camp.
  • Stage 3 (Camp 2): The top 25 participants from Camp 1 advance to the second camp.
  • Stage 4 (Olympiad Team): The final top students are selected to represent Pakistan internationally.
