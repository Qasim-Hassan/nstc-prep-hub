# 🚀 NSTC Prep Hub

An open-source, educational platform designed to help Pakistani high school students prepare for the National Science Talent Contest (NSTC) and secure a spot in the International Olympiads .

🔗 **Live Demo:** https://olympiadhub-pk.netlify.app/

## 🧠 The Mission
Access to NSTC training materials is often scattered or hard to access . The NSTC Prep Hub solves this by providing a centralized, interactive,  ecosystem where students can access syllabus roadmaps, test their mastery, and share resources openly.

## ✨ Key Features
* **Live Community Board:** A real-time, database driven directory where candidates can upload and share Google Drive/Notion links for notes.
* **Olympiad Oracle (AI Integration):** An chatbot powered by Google's Gemma 4B model to answer syllabus and Olympiad related questions.
* **UI/UX:** Dark-mode UI for better readability during long study sessions.
